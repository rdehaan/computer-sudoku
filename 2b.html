Speed is not always enough

Now let's turn to the really hard example: Sudoku B. As you can see in the demonstration below, the backtracking algorithm is not intelligent enough to solve this Sudoku puzzle (really) quickly. Even on the maximum speed, the demonstration will take a while.

Of course, you could construct a more efficient implementation of this algorithm (the demonstration in your browser is not the fastest). The main point here is that this algorithm still has to go through quite a number of steps to get to the solution.
